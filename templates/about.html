About Us

Wheelhouse Bike is a reputable bike-selling company known for its high-quality products and exceptional customer service. They offer a wide range of bicycles catering to various styles, including road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, and more. With a knowledgeable and friendly staff, Wheelhouse Bike assists customers in selecting the perfect bike to match their needs and preferences. The company prioritizes customer satisfaction and ensures that each purchase delivers excellent value for money. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a beginner, Wheelhouse Bike is committed to providing a positive and rewarding experience for all bike enthusiasts. Wheelhouse Bike will provide you a software solution which will predict you the efficient price to buy your bike. We offer a comprehensive database of Bike information and use proprietary algorithms to estimate the market value of used bikes. Our software takes into account factors such as the bike's condition, age brand, and components to determine an accurate price range.
